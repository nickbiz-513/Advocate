01 — Profile

Professional Background

Alexander Sterling was called to the Bar in 2001 after graduating with honours from the Global University School of Law — one of the foremost institutions for legal education. He enrolled with the State Legal Council and has since practised extensively before the Federal Supreme Court, the State High Court, and several statutory tribunals.

His practice encompasses the full spectrum of commercial litigation, with particular depth in insolvency proceedings under the Insolvency Code, corporate advisory mandates, and constitutional matters. He has appeared in several matters of national significance before the constitutional benches of the Federal Supreme Court.

He is a member of the Federal Supreme Court Bar Association and the State High Court Bar Association, and is empanelled as an arbitrator with the Metropolis International Arbitration Centre.
