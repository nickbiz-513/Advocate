01 — Profile

Professional Background

Arjun S. Mehta was called to the Bar in 2001 after graduating with honours from the National Law School of India University, Bengaluru — one of India's foremost institutions for legal education. He enrolled with the Bar Council of Delhi and has since practised extensively before the Supreme Court of India, the Delhi High Court, and several statutory tribunals.

His practice encompasses the full spectrum of commercial litigation, with particular depth in insolvency proceedings under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), corporate advisory mandates, and constitutional matters. He has appeared in several matters of national significance before the constitutional benches of the Supreme Court.

He is a member of the Supreme Court Bar Association and the Delhi High Court Bar Association, and is empanelled as an arbitrator with the Delhi International Arbitration Centre (DIAC).
